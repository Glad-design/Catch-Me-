3. GLAD PARROT!

This is a game of helping a life of any animal like parrot, frog...

Its a LOOP GAME which focuses on score, NO END!

You can take many score as you can!

HOW TO PLAY?

An animal is on the way home after is morning walk.

But one day its route to home was wrong and its traped on a place full of wall.

Those walls are very dainger, if that animal touches on that wall then GAME OVER.

The animal is on the AIR, you can let it go upwords and downwords.

On mobile you can use the HUD of UP and DOWN arrow to let it go UP and DOWNWORDS.

If you are using PC, you can turn off the HUD from the settings and you can use UP and DOWN arrows or scroll UP and DOWN to control the animal.
